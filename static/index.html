Twitter2FF
v1.0

Written by Carter Rabasa [web] [ff] [t]

Twitter2FF is a tool for syncing your Twitter and FriendFeed friends. Just enter your credentials and we'll retrieve your friend lists, cross-check them, and give you the option to follow or subscribe directly from this page! We do not store any of your information. You can read more about how Twitter2FF works here and here.
